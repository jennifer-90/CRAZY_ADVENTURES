    ** JUMANJI **

    Jumanji Safari: Embark on a safari through the jungle and face the challenges of Jumanji. You will start by equipping yourself with a backpack filled with survival supplies and will have to face various challenges throughout your journey. You can build shelter, hunt for your food, navigate through dangerous waterways, and encounter wild animals. The goal is to survive in the Jumanji jungle while having fun with your friends or family. Of course, it's important to respect the animals and the environment while enjoying this adventure inspired by the famous movie.
